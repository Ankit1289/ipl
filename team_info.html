About Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians (MI) is one of the most successful franchises in the Indian Premier League (IPL), founded in 2008 and owned by Reliance Industries. With their home ground at Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai, the team has built a legacy of consistency and excellence.
